    About KFS

    KFS was founded in 2024. Our shop was built from a love of street food and a passion for serving authentic South African cuisine to our community in Johannesburg.

    SOUTH AFRICAN STREET FOOD TRIVIA

    WHAT IS A KOTA?

    Similar to a bunny chow, a "quarter" [kota] is a quarter of bread stuffed with ingredients like soft fries, polony, fried egg, cheese, and atchar. It's a beloved street food across South Africa!

    WHY SOUTH AFRICAN STREET FOOD?

    South African street food represents the vibrant culture and diverse flavors of our nation. At KFS, we celebrate these traditions by serving authentic recipes with quality ingredients.
